## [1.0.0] — 2026-04-01

### 🎉 Initial Release

#### Core Features
- **12-Phase execution protocol** — from product discovery to post-launch monitoring
- **Product Discovery Engine (Phase 0A)** — 8-question intake, web search, popular + hidden gem recommendations with personalised Top Pick
- **Global location intelligence** — fully adapts to any of 50+ countries; currency, regulations, trade blocs, funding all localised
- **13-document advisory package** across 3 tiers

#### Tier 1 — Role-Based Research
- DOC 1: Market Intelligence Report (Market Research Analyst voice)
- DOC 2: Business Strategy Brief (Strategist voice)
- DOC 3: Financial Model & Projections (Financial Analyst voice)
- DOC 4: Trade Compliance Brief (Regulatory Advisor voice)
- DOC 5: Sourcing & Supply Chain Plan (Supply Chain Specialist voice)
- DOC 6: Risk Assessment Report (Risk Advisor voice)

#### Tier 2 — Master Documents
- DOC 7: Executive Master Summary (HTML dashboard — internal use only)
- DOC 8: Investor & Bank Pitch Pack (15-slide narrative)

#### Tier 3 — Outreach & Communication
- DOC 9: One-Page Business Snapshot (print-ready HTML)
- DOC 10: Cold Email Templates (3 variants + follow-ups)
- DOC 11: WhatsApp Message Scripts (5 templates)
- DOC 12: LinkedIn & B2B Portal Messages (4 templates)
- DOC 13: Trade Fair Elevator Pitch Scripts (3 versions)

#### Reference Library (13 files)
- `global-trade-compliance.md` — 50+ countries with full regulatory detail
- `funding-directory.md` — 50+ global funders by region
- `financial-model.md` — Universal financial model framework
- `sourcing-guide.md` — Global sourcing maps and supplier due diligence
- `risk-assessment.md` — Risk scoring and contingency playbooks
- `negotiation-playbook.md` — 6 negotiation domains covered
- `legal-contracts.md` — 5 contract templates
- `digital-presence.md` — Complete online setup guide
- `change-management.md` — Version control and amendment system
- `performance-monitoring.md` — Monthly review and scaling frameworks
- `failure-exit-protocol.md` — Triage, wind-down, and reset playbook
- `document-styling.md` — Visual design system for all documents

#### Special Features
- Quick-Access Menu — jump to any phase directly
- Change Management System (Phase 11) — amendment vs. full revision
- Post-Launch Monitoring (Phase 12) — monthly review, scaling, pivot, exit
- Visual document styling — navy/gold cover pages, colour-coded tables, HTML dashboards
