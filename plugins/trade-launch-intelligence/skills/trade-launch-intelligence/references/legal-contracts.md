# Legal Contracts Starter Pack — Trade Launch Intelligence
# Simple, Usable Contract Templates for Traders & Exporters

## IMPORTANT DISCLAIMER
These templates are starting points only. For any contract involving
amounts above $10,000 USD (or local equivalent), have a qualified
trade lawyer in your country review before signing. Laws vary
significantly by country and product type.

## 1. SUPPLY AGREEMENT
*Use when: You are buying from a local supplier regularly*
*Protects you from: Price disputes, quality failure, supply default*

---

**SUPPLY AGREEMENT**

This Supply Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into on [Date]
between:

**BUYER:** [Your Company Name], registered at [address], hereinafter
referred to as "the Buyer"

**SUPPLIER:** [Supplier Name / Company], registered at [address],
hereinafter referred to as "the Supplier"

**1. PRODUCT**
The Supplier agrees to supply [full product name] ("the Product")
meeting the following specifications:
- Grade/Quality: [Specify — e.g., Grade A, KOR 48+, moisture max 9%]
- Packaging: [e.g., 80kg jute bags, clean and new]
- Origin: [State/Region/Country of origin]
- Any other specifications: [list]

**2. VOLUME**
The Buyer agrees to purchase a minimum of [X] metric tons per [month/
quarter] and a maximum of [Y] metric tons, subject to the Buyer's
confirmed purchase orders.

**3. PRICE**
3.1 The price per metric ton shall be [₦X / $X] [fixed / or linked to
[market index] ± [%] at time of order confirmation].
3.2 Price shall be reviewed [monthly / quarterly] by mutual agreement.
3.3 No price changes shall apply to orders already confirmed in writing.

**4. PAYMENT**
4.1 Payment shall be made [on delivery / within [X] days of delivery /
30% advance + 70% on delivery].
4.2 Payment method: [Bank transfer / cash / mobile money].
4.3 Late payment will attract interest of [X]% per week after [X] days.

**5. DELIVERY**
5.1 Delivery point: [Buyer's warehouse / Supplier's location / named location]
5.2 Delivery timeline: Within [X] days of confirmed purchase order.
5.3 Risk of loss passes to Buyer upon delivery at agreed delivery point.

**6. QUALITY & INSPECTION**
6.1 The Buyer reserves the right to inspect and test the Product before
payment or within [3] business days of delivery.
6.2 If the Product fails to meet agreed specifications, the Buyer may:
    (a) Reject the full batch and demand replacement within [X] days, or
    (b) Accept at a price reduction agreed in writing.
6.3 Testing shall be conducted by [an accredited lab / agreed third party].

**7. FORCE MAJEURE**
Neither party shall be liable for delay or failure caused by events
beyond their reasonable control (natural disaster, government action,
conflict) provided written notice is given within [7] days of the event.

**8. TERM & TERMINATION**
8.1 This Agreement is effective for [6 months / 1 year] from the date above.
8.2 Either party may terminate with [30] days written notice.
8.3 Termination does not affect rights arising from orders already confirmed.

**9. DISPUTE RESOLUTION**
9.1 Parties shall first attempt to resolve disputes through negotiation
within [14] days of a written notice of dispute.
9.2 If unresolved, disputes shall be referred to [arbitration under
[country's Arbitration Act] / the courts of [jurisdiction]].

**10. ENTIRE AGREEMENT**
This Agreement supersedes all prior discussions, emails, or verbal
agreements between the parties on this subject.

Signed:

BUYER: _________________________ Date: _____________
Name: [Name] | Title: [Title] | Company: [Company]

SUPPLIER: ______________________ Date: _____________
Name: [Name] | Title: [Title] | Company: [Company]

Witness: _______________________ Date: _____________

## 2. SALES / PURCHASE CONTRACT (EXPORT)
*Use when: You are selling to an international buyer*
*Protects you from: Non-payment, quality dispute, delivery terms dispute*

---

**INTERNATIONAL SALES CONTRACT**

CONTRACT No.: [Your reference number]
Date: [Date]

**SELLER:** [Your Company Name]
[Registration Number] | [Address] | [Country]
Contact: [Name] | Email: [Email] | Tel: [Phone]

**BUYER:** [Buyer Company Name]
[Registration Number] | [Address] | [Country]
Contact: [Name] | Email: [Email] | Tel: [Phone]

**1. PRODUCT DESCRIPTION**
| Item | Detail |
|---|---|
| Product Name | [Full name] |
| HS Code | [6-digit code] |
| Quality/Grade | [Specification] |
| Packaging | [Description] |
| Country of Origin | [Country] |
| Additional specs | [Any other agreed specs] |

**2. QUANTITY**
[X] metric tons / [X] units
Tolerance: +/- [5]% at Seller's option

**3. PRICE**
Unit Price: USD $[X] per metric ton / per unit
Total Contract Value: USD $[X]
Basis: [FOB / CIF / CFR / DDP] [Port/Location name]
(Incoterms [2020])

**4. PAYMENT TERMS**
☐ Option A: 100% Telegraphic Transfer (TT) in advance
☐ Option B: [X]% TT advance + balance [X]% against copy of BL
☐ Option C: Irrevocable Letter of Credit (LC)
   - Issuing Bank: [Bank name]
   - LC validity: [X days after shipment date]
   - Documents required: [list as agreed]
☐ Option D: Other: [specify]

**5. SHIPMENT**
5.1 Port of Loading: [Port name, Country]
5.2 Port of Discharge: [Port name, Country]
5.3 Latest Shipment Date: [Date]
5.4 Partial Shipments: Permitted / Not Permitted
5.5 Transhipment: Permitted / Not Permitted
5.6 Shipping Line: [As arranged by Seller/Buyer per Incoterms]

**6. DOCUMENTS**
Seller shall provide the following documents:
- [ ] Commercial Invoice (3 originals)
- [ ] Packing List (3 originals)
- [ ] Full set of original Bills of Lading
- [ ] Certificate of Origin (issued by [authority])
- [ ] Phytosanitary Certificate (if applicable)
- [ ] Quality/Inspection Certificate ([SGS/BV/other])
- [ ] Fumigation Certificate (if applicable)
- [ ] Marine Insurance Certificate (if CIF)
- [ ] Other: [specify]

**7. QUALITY & INSPECTION**
7.1 Product quality shall conform to specifications in Clause 1.
7.2 Pre-shipment inspection shall be conducted by [SGS / Bureau Veritas /
    Cotecna / other agreed party] at Seller's / Buyer's expense.
7.3 Inspection certificate shall be final and binding on both parties
    regarding quality and quantity at time of shipment.
7.4 Claims for quality must be made within [14] days of arrival at
    destination, supported by an independent survey report.

**8. FORCE MAJEURE**
[Same clause as Supply Agreement above]

**9. GOVERNING LAW & DISPUTE RESOLUTION**
9.1 This contract shall be governed by the laws of [Seller's country /
    agreed neutral jurisdiction].
9.2 Disputes shall first be resolved by negotiation within [21] days.
9.3 If unresolved: [ICC Arbitration / local arbitration / named court].

**10. ENTIRE AGREEMENT**
This contract, together with any attached product specifications,
constitutes the entire agreement between the parties.

Signed for SELLER: _________________ Date: _______
Signed for BUYER: __________________ Date: _______

## 3. NON-DISCLOSURE AGREEMENT (NDA)
*Use when: Sharing your business plan, financial model, or sourcing
details with potential investors, partners, or buyers*

---

**MUTUAL NON-DISCLOSURE AGREEMENT**

This Agreement is entered into on [Date] between:
Party A: [Your Company Name], [Country]
Party B: [Recipient Name / Company], [Country]

**1. PURPOSE**
The parties wish to explore a potential business relationship
("Purpose") and may share confidential information with each other.

**2. CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION**
Includes: business plans, financial projections, supplier details,
customer lists, pricing, trade secrets, and any other information
marked as confidential or which a reasonable person would consider
confidential.

**3. OBLIGATIONS**
Each party shall:
(a) Keep all confidential information strictly confidential
(b) Not disclose to any third party without prior written consent
(c) Use only for the Purpose stated above
(d) Apply the same care as for its own confidential information
    (minimum reasonable care)

**4. EXCLUSIONS**
Obligations do not apply to information that:
(a) Was already publicly known at the time of disclosure
(b) Becomes public through no breach of this Agreement
(c) Was independently developed without use of confidential information
(d) Is required to be disclosed by law (with prior notice where possible)

**5. TERM**
This Agreement remains in effect for [2 years] from the date above,
or until the Purpose is concluded, whichever is earlier.
Confidentiality obligations survive termination for a further [2 years].

**6. REMEDIES**
The parties acknowledge that breach may cause irreparable harm for
which damages alone would be inadequate, and injunctive relief may
be sought without the need to post bond.

**7. GOVERNING LAW**
[Agreed jurisdiction — typically one party's country or neutral]

Party A: _________________________ Date: _______
Party B: _________________________ Date: _______

## 4. LETTER OF INTENT (LOI)
*Use when: A buyer wants to confirm interest before a formal contract*
*Note: An LOI is NOT a binding purchase commitment in most jurisdictions*

---

**LETTER OF INTENT TO PURCHASE**

Date: [Date]
From: [Buyer Company Name and Address]
To: [Seller Company Name and Address]

Re: Letter of Intent — Purchase of [Product Name]

Dear [Seller Name],

This Letter of Intent ("LOI") confirms [Buyer Company]'s genuine
interest in purchasing [product] from [Seller Company] subject to
the following preliminary terms:

| Term | Detail |
|---|---|
| Product | [Name, grade, specification] |
| Quantity | [X] metric tons (indicative) |
| Indicative Price | USD $[X] per ton [FOB/CIF] |
| Delivery Period | [Month/Quarter/Year] |
| Payment | [Payment terms — TT/LC] |
| Port of Destination | [Port name, Country] |

This LOI:
- Confirms our genuine intent to enter into a formal purchase contract
- Does NOT constitute a binding commitment to purchase
- Is subject to satisfactory quality samples, regulatory approvals,
  and final contract negotiation
- Is valid until [date]

We look forward to progressing this to a formal contract.

Signed: _________________________ Date: _______
Name/Title: [Name, Title]
Company: [Buyer Company]

## 5. SIMPLE AGENT / COMMISSION AGREEMENT
*Use when: Someone introduces buyers to you in exchange for commission*

---

**SALES COMMISSION AGREEMENT**

Date: [Date]
Between:
Principal: [Your Company Name] ("the Company")
Agent: [Agent Name / Company] ("the Agent")

**1. APPOINTMENT**
The Company appoints the Agent as a non-exclusive sales agent for
the territory of [Country/Region] for the following products:
[Product list]

**2. COMMISSION**
The Agent shall receive [X]% of the FOB/CIF invoice value of any
sale concluded with a buyer introduced by the Agent.
Commission is payable within [30] days of receipt of buyer payment.

**3. AGENT'S OBLIGATIONS**
The Agent shall:
- Actively promote the Company's products in the territory
- Not make commitments on price or terms without written approval
- Report buyer contacts and progress [monthly / on request]
- Not represent competing suppliers without written consent

**4. COMPANY'S OBLIGATIONS**
The Company shall:
- Provide current product specs, pricing, and availability
- Notify the Agent promptly of any changes to products or pricing
- Pay commission as agreed without undue delay

**5. TERM & TERMINATION**
Agreement runs for [1 year], renewable by mutual consent.
Either party may terminate with [30 days] written notice.
Commission earned before termination remains payable.

**6. INDEPENDENT CONTRACTOR**
The Agent is an independent contractor — not an employee. The Agent
has no authority to bind the Company to any contract.

**7. GOVERNING LAW**
[Jurisdiction]

Principal: _________________________ Date: _______
Agent: ____________________________ Date: _______

## 6. KEY CLAUSES REFERENCE LIBRARY

### Force Majeure (standard wording)
"Neither party shall be liable for failure or delay in performance
caused by events beyond its reasonable control, including but not
limited to acts of God, war, government action, pandemic, natural
disaster, port closure, or transport disruption, provided that the
affected party gives written notice within [7] days of the event
and uses reasonable efforts to resume performance."

### Jurisdiction (seller-friendly)
"This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of [Seller's Country].
Any dispute shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the
courts of [Seller's City, Country]."

### Jurisdiction (neutral — recommended for international deals)
"Any dispute arising out of this Agreement shall be finally settled
by arbitration under the Rules of the International Chamber of
Commerce (ICC), with seat of arbitration in [neutral city e.g.
London, Singapore, Dubai]. The language of arbitration shall be English."

### Entire Agreement
"This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties
with respect to the subject matter hereof, and supersedes all prior
negotiations, representations, or agreements, whether written or oral."

### No Waiver
"Failure to enforce any provision of this Agreement shall not
constitute a waiver of future enforcement of that or any other provision."

### Severability
"If any provision of this Agreement is held invalid or unenforceable,
the remaining provisions shall continue in full force and effect."

## 7. CONTRACT RED FLAGS — WARNING SIGNS

Watch for these in contracts sent to you by buyers, suppliers, or partners:

### 🔴 High-Risk Red Flags (refuse or get lawyer immediately)
- Contract law is the buyer's home country only (you have no standing to sue there)
- Payment on "acceptance" with no defined acceptance criteria
- Unlimited liability clause on your side
- Buyer can cancel for any reason after goods are shipped
- Inspection certificate can be issued by the buyer themselves
- Confidentiality clause that prevents you from disclosing fraud

### 🟡 Medium-Risk Red Flags (negotiate before signing)
- Penalty for late delivery but no penalty for late payment
- Dispute resolution by a body you've never heard of
- Quality claim window longer than 30 days
- Contract automatically renews without notice
- Price adjustment clauses that only work in buyer's favour
- Jurisdiction in a country with weak contract enforcement

### 🟢 Normal — Don't Worry About These
- Force majeure clause (standard)
- Entire agreement clause (standard)
- No waiver clause (standard)
- Confidentiality of contract terms (normal)
- Right to assign contract with consent (normal)
