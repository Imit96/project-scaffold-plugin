# Document Templates Reference — Trade Launch Intelligence
# Role-Based Advisory Package (8 Documents)

## Architecture Overview

```
TIER 1 — ROLE-SPECIFIC DELIVERABLES
├── DOC 1: Market Intelligence Report        [Market Research Analyst]
├── DOC 2: Business Strategy Brief           [Business Strategist]
├── DOC 3: Financial Model & Projections     [Financial Analyst]
├── DOC 4: Trade Compliance Brief            [Regulatory/Compliance Advisor]
├── DOC 5: Sourcing & Supply Chain Plan      [Supply Chain Specialist]
└── DOC 6: Risk Assessment Report            [Risk Advisor]

TIER 2 — MASTER DOCUMENTS
├── DOC 7: Executive Master Summary          [CEO-level synthesis of all 6]
└── DOC 8: Investor & Bank Pitch Pack        [Pitch deck + financial highlights]
```

**Generation Rule:** Always produce all 8 documents. Each document is self-contained — a
recipient should be able to read it without needing any other document. Each is written in
the professional voice of its role. Never mix roles within a single document.

**Localisation Rule:** Replace ALL currency symbols, regulatory body names, and country-
specific references with those appropriate to the user's declared country. Never leave
Nigeria-specific references for non-Nigerian users.
