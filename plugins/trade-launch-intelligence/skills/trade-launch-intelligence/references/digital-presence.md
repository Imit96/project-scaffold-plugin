## 1. DOMAIN & PROFESSIONAL EMAIL
*Priority: 🔴 Must have before any outreach*

### Domain Registration
- **What:** Your web address — e.g., `agroexportsng.com`
- **Where to register:** Namecheap (cheapest), GoDaddy, Google Domains
- **Cost:** $8–$15/year for `.com` | $3–$8/year for country domains (.ng, .gh, .ke, .in etc.)
- **Tips:**
  - Always get the `.com` if available — it signals credibility globally
  - Keep it short, pronounceable, and related to your product or company
  - Avoid hyphens (agroexport-ng.com looks spammy)
  - Register for 2+ years upfront (signals stability to buyers)

### Professional Email
- **What:** `yourname@yourcompany.com` — NOT gmail.com or yahoo.com
- **Why:** Using Gmail for business communication loses you 30–40% of serious buyers
- **Where:** Google Workspace ($6/month), Zoho Mail (free for 5 users), Microsoft 365 ($6/month)
- **Setup:** Buy domain first, then connect to email provider
- **Cost:** $0–$6/month
- **Recommended setup:**
  - `info@yourcompany.com` — general enquiries
  - `yourname@yourcompany.com` — personal outreach
  - `sales@yourcompany.com` — B2B portals

## 2. BASIC BUSINESS WEBSITE
*Priority: 🟡 Important within first 30 days*

### What Your Website Must Have (Minimum Viable)
A trade business website does NOT need to be fancy. It needs 5 things:

1. **Who you are** — Company name, country, what you trade
2. **What you sell** — Product name, quality spec, certifications, photos
3. **Why trust you** — Certifications, team photo, brief story, any testimonials
4. **How to reach you** — Email, WhatsApp, phone, physical address
5. **A call to action** — "Request a Sample" or "Get a Quote" button

### Build Options by Budget

| Option | Cost | Skill Needed | Best For |
|---|---|---|---|
| Wix / Squarespace | $16–$23/month | None | Complete beginners |
| WordPress.com | $4–$25/month | Basic | Most traders |
| WordPress.org + Hosting | $5–$10/month hosting | Moderate | More control |
| Webflow | $16/month | Moderate | Professional look |
| Hire a developer (local) | $200–$800 one-off | None | If budget available |

### Must-Have Pages
- **Home:** Hero image of product, 1-line description, contact button
- **Products:** Each product with: photo, grade/spec, certifications, origin, minimum order
- **About Us:** Company story (2–3 paragraphs), team photo, country/location
- **Certifications:** Photos or PDFs of all certifications and quality documents
- **Contact:** Form + email + WhatsApp link + phone + address

### SEO Basics (so buyers can find you)
- Page title: "[Product] Exporter from [Country] | [Company Name]"
- Include product name + country in page text naturally
- Add alt text to all product images
- Register on Google Search Console (free) to track visibility

## 3. WHATSAPP BUSINESS
*Priority: 🔴 Must have — especially in Africa, Asia, Middle East*

### Setup Checklist
- [ ] Download WhatsApp Business app (free — separate from personal WhatsApp)
- [ ] Use a dedicated SIM/number for business (not your personal number)
- [ ] Business name: [Company Name]
- [ ] Profile photo: Company logo or clean product photo
- [ ] Business description: "[Product] exporter based in [Country]. Supply [Grade] to international buyers. [Cert names]."
- [ ] Business category: Agriculture / Manufacturing / Wholesale & Distribution
- [ ] Email: [business email]
- [ ] Website: [your website]
- [ ] Address: [City, Country]
- [ ] Business hours: Set realistic hours

### Catalogue Setup
Add your products to the WhatsApp catalogue:
- Product name + description
- Price (or "Price on request")
- High-quality photo (natural light, clean background)
- Product code/SKU

### Automated Messages
Set up these 3 automated messages:
1. **Welcome message** (when someone messages first):
   "Hello! Thanks for reaching out to [Company]. We export [product] from [country]. How can we help you today?"

2. **Away message** (outside business hours):
   "Thanks for your message. We'll respond within [X hours] during business hours ([hours], [timezone]). For urgent enquiries: [email]."

3. **Quick replies** (for common questions):
   - `/price` → "Our current FOB price for [product] is $[X] per [unit]. MOQ: [X] tons. Would you like a formal quotation?"
   - `/cert` → "We hold: [list certifications]. Certificates available on request."
   - `/sample` → "Samples available. Minimum 500g. Buyer covers courier cost. Send your address and we'll arrange."

## 4. LINKEDIN
*Priority: 🟡 Important for international credibility*

### Personal Profile (The Founder)
Your personal LinkedIn is often the first thing an international buyer or investor checks.

**Headline:** Not just your job title. Example:
"Founder, [Company] | [Product] Exporter from [Country] | Supplying EU, UAE & Asia Markets"

**About section (first 3 lines are visible without clicking):**
"I founded [Company] to connect [country]'s premium [product] with international buyers who value quality and consistency. We export [grade] [product] to [markets], with full [certifications]. Currently seeking buyers and distribution partners in [regions]."

**Experience:** Add your company as current position with full description

**Featured section:** Add your product spec sheet or DOC 9 (One-Page Snapshot) as PDF

**Activity:** Post one update per week — product updates, certifications obtained, shipment milestones, market news

### LinkedIn Company Page
- Company name + logo
- Tagline: "[Product] Export | [Country] | [Key certification]"
- About: 3–5 paragraphs on what you do, where you source, what you certify, who you sell to
- Location: Your city and country
- Industry: Agriculture / Manufacturing / Import and Export
- Website: [your website]
- Post updates: same content as personal page, 2× per month minimum

### LinkedIn Outreach Strategy
- Connect with 5–10 new relevant contacts per week (buyers, importers in target countries)
- Engage with their posts (like, comment) before sending a message
- Use DOC 12 (LinkedIn message templates) for outreach
- Join trade groups: "International Trade Network", "[product] buyers", "[country] exporters"

## 5. B2B TRADE PORTAL PROFILES
*Priority: 🔴 Critical for export businesses*

### Tier 1 — Set Up Immediately (Free)

**Alibaba.com (as supplier)**
- Create free supplier account
- Complete company verification (takes 3–7 days)
- Add product listings: title, specifications, certifications, photos, price range
- Enable: Trade Assurance (builds buyer trust)
- Target: Buyers in Europe, USA, Middle East, Southeast Asia

**TradeKey.com**
- Free basic profile
- Good for Middle East, Pakistan, Bangladesh buyers
- Post products + respond to RFQs (Request for Quotation)

**EC21.com**
- Good for Korean and Asian buyers
- Free basic listing

**Kompass.com**
- European and global B2B directory
- Free basic profile — good for EU buyer discovery
- Paid Premium: $50–$200/month (wait until you have consistent leads)

### Tier 2 — Add Within 60 Days

**Global Sources** (for manufactured goods, electronics, hardware)
**Made-in-China.com** (alternative to Alibaba for some categories)
**Tridge.com** (agri-food — partially free, good price intelligence too)
**Europages.com** (European focus — good for EU market entry)

### Profile Optimisation Rules (for all portals)
- Use your real company name — not vague names like "Best Quality Trade Co"
- Upload high-resolution product photos (minimum 5 per product)
- Add ALL certifications as visible documents
- Fill in every field — incomplete profiles rank lower
- Include response rate commitment — respond within 24 hours
- Use keywords buyers search: "[product] exporter [country]", "Grade A [product]", "[product] certified organic"

## 6. GOOGLE BUSINESS PROFILE
*Priority: 🟢 Nice to have — helps local and international credibility*

- Free to create at business.google.com
- Appears in Google Maps and Google search results
- Add: company name, address, phone, website, business category, photos, description
- Particularly useful if you have a physical warehouse or office buyers might visit
- Collect Google reviews from anyone who visits or does business with you

## 7. SOCIAL MEDIA — WHAT'S WORTH IT

For trade businesses, social media ROI varies significantly. Be selective.

| Platform | Worth It? | Why | How Often |
|---|---|---|---|
| LinkedIn | ✅ Yes | B2B buyers are here | 2–3× per week |
| WhatsApp Business | ✅ Yes | Primary comms in developing markets | Daily |
| Instagram | 🟡 Maybe | Good for visual products (cosmetics, food, crafts) | 2× per week if visual |
| Facebook | 🟡 Maybe | Useful in West Africa, Southeast Asia | 2× per week if relevant |
| Twitter/X | ❌ Skip | Low ROI for trade businesses | — |
| TikTok | ❌ Skip | Wrong audience for B2B trade | — |
| YouTube | 🟡 Maybe | Farm/factory/process tour videos build huge trust | Monthly if possible |

**Most underrated content for trade businesses:**
Short video (60 seconds) walking through your farm, warehouse, or processing facility.
Nothing builds international buyer trust like seeing where the product comes from.
Film on a smartphone. Post on LinkedIn and WhatsApp. Costs nothing.

## 9. COST SUMMARY BY BUDGET LEVEL

### Starter Budget ($20–$50/month)
- Domain: $10/year = $1/month
- Email (Zoho free or Google Workspace basic): $0–$6/month
- Website (Wix/WordPress basic): $8–$16/month
- WhatsApp Business: Free
- B2B portals (Alibaba, TradeKey, EC21): Free basic
- LinkedIn: Free
- **Total: ~$15–$25/month**

### Growth Budget ($100–$200/month)
- Everything above +
- Google Workspace (professional email + storage): $6/month
- Website (better plan or developer): $25–$50/month
- Alibaba Gold Supplier upgrade: $150–$300/month (wait until leads justify)
- Kompass Premium: $50–$100/month
- **Total: ~$80–$160/month**

### Scale Budget ($300+/month)
- Everything above +
- Alibaba Gold Supplier verified: $299/month
- Dedicated web developer for updates
- LinkedIn Premium (Sales Navigator): $79/month
- PR/content writer for trade media
- **Total: $300–$600/month**

**Recommendation for first-time traders:** Start with the $20–$50/month setup.
Get your first buyer and first payment BEFORE spending on premium memberships.
Proof of sales converts much faster than a polished website alone.
